Apple Watch Series 2 is the second-generation models of the Apple Watch and were released on September 16, 2016.The Series 2 has a built-in GPS and twice the display brightness of the previous generation for easier outdoor readability. The Apple Watch Series 1 and 2 requires at least an iPhone 5 with iOS 10 or later. Apple Watch Series 2 introduces water resistance at a rating of 50 meters. It can keep track of the number of laps and distance during Swimming workouts. During swimming, the touch function can be manually disabled with 'Underwater mode'.
