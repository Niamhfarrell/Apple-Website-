IOS

iOS (formerly iPhone OS) is a mobile operating system created and developed by Apple Inc. exclusively for its hardware. It is the operating system that powers many of the company's mobile devices, including the iPhone and iPod TouchMajor versions of iOS are released annually. The current version, iOS 14, was released on September 2020. It brought many changes, to place widgets on the home screen, a compact UI for both Siri and phone calls, and the ability to change both the default web browser and email apps.
